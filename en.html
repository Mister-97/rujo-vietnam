About Rujo

A New Kind of
Coffee Experience

Rujo Coffee House Vietnam is a creative coffee concept built around unique drink experiences, specialty blends, and curated pop-up events. We blend Vietnamese ingredients, global inspiration, and innovative drink design to create moments worth remembering.

In Vietnam, Rujo operates as a pop-up beverage experience and drink development studio — not a fixed café. We bring the coffee to you.

01

Sourced with care — Vietnamese robusta, tropical fruits, and imported specialty components.

02

03

Memorable Experiences

We don't just serve coffee — we create moments at events, markets, and private gatherings.

Signature Drinks

Crafted to Be Remembered

Each drink is a concept — built from cultural inspiration, premium ingredients, and the belief that coffee can be art.

Signature

Portuguese Egg Tart Coffee

Inspired by the classic Portuguese pastel de nata. Custard-smooth, lightly caramelized and rich in a way that hits different from anything you'd expect in a cup.

Signature

Red Thai Coconut Milk Tea

Layered Thai tea over smooth coconut milk. The color alone stops people in their tracks and the taste keeps them coming back for a second cup.

House Blend

Peanut Kreme Coffee

Nutty, creamy and unexpectedly smooth. Peanut kreme meets cold coffee in a way that feels indulgent without being heavy. One of the most ordered at every pop-up.

Pop-Up Events

We Go Where the Moment Is

Rujo Coffee House regularly hosts and participates in pop-up coffee experiences across Vietnam — bringing creative drinks and specialty coffee to unique gatherings and celebrations.

Sports Tournaments

Our in-house R&D lab crafts premium premix formulas and signature drink recipes ready for retail, wholesale, and brand licensing. From concept to production-ready blend.

Community Gatherings

Private Celebrations
